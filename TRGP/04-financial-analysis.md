## Company Profile

Targa Resources is one of the largest independent midstream infrastructure companies in North America. The company operates in two segments:

1. **Gathering and Processing** -- gathering, compressing, treating, processing, and selling natural gas and crude oil, primarily in the Permian Basin (West Texas/Southeast New Mexico), Eagle Ford Shale, Barnett Shale, Anadarko/Ardmore/Arkoma Basins, Williston Basin, and Louisiana Gulf Coast.

2. **Logistics and Transportation** -- transporting, fractionating, storing, terminaling, and marketing NGLs and NGL products, including LPG export services. Key assets at Mont Belvieu and Galena Park, Texas, and Lake Charles, Louisiana.

## Key Risks

1. **Permian production growth deceleration** -- 80% of G&P inlet volumes are Permian-sourced
2. **NGL price and demand risk** -- commodity exposure remains meaningful
3. **Capital program execution** -- $3.3B+ annual growth capex with 10+ major projects active
4. **Interest rate and leverage risk** -- $852.8M annual interest expense, heavy growth funding needs
5. **Regulatory risk** -- emissions regulation, pipeline permitting, potential export restrictions
6. **Customer/producer concentration** -- E&P consolidation increases counterparty bargaining power
